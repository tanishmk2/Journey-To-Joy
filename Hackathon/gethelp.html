KIRAN
24/7 Toll free helpline

The Union Social Justice and Empowerment Ministry
launched a 24/7 toll-free helpline to provide support to people
facing anxiety, stress, depression, suicidal thoughts, and other mental health concerns.
The mental health rehabilitation helpline, KIRAN,
can be called from a landline and mobile phones across the country at the number 1800-599-0019.
